Trust & safety

How escrow works

1 · Buyer pays

Card, crypto, or site credit. The money lands in Vault escrow — the seller can see it's secured, but can't touch it yet.

2 · Trade in Roblox

Buyer and seller coordinate through order chat and complete the item hand-off in game. The seller marks it delivered.

3 · Buyer confirms

Once the buyer confirms receipt (or 72h pass after delivery with no dispute), escrow releases to the seller's balance.

4 · Something wrong?

Open a dispute — payment freezes instantly and a moderator reviews the order chat to refund or release.
